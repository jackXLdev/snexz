HighLevel (GHL) is engineered specifically for marketing agencies. It is designed to replace a dozen separate tools—CRM, landing page builder, email marketing, calendar booking, and SMS—with a single, unified suite.

Its killer feature is the ability to fully white-label the software and resell it to your own clients under your agency's domain name. While it offers incredible cost savings through consolidation, the sheer volume of features means it has a notably steep learning curve.

Operational Advantages
