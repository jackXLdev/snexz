Apollo.io is a powerhouse for B2B sales teams, combining a massive B2B contact database with robust sales engagement tools. It allows SDRs and AEs to find ideal prospects, enrich data, and execute multi-channel outreach sequences all from a single tab.

While the platform offers immense value and consolidates the tech stack by merging data and engagement, the sheer volume of features can overwhelm new users. Data accuracy is generally high, though it may vary depending on how niche the industry is.
