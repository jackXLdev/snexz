Snexz Report

Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) disrupts traditional marketing SaaS by charging per email sent rather than per contact stored. This makes it a massively cost-effective engine for businesses with large, dormant, or slowly growing audience lists.

Alongside standard newsletters, it excels at API-driven transactional emails (like password resets or receipts) and SMS marketing. While its automation builder isn't as deep as premium enterprise tools, it is more than capable of handling standard drip campaigns, cart abandonments, and basic CRM workflows.

Operational Advantages

Unlimited contact storage on all plans

Excellent transactional email API

Built-in sales CRM functionality

Known Limitations

Automations are slightly basic

Template editor can be rigid
