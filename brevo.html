Snexz Report

Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) disrupted the email marketing space by adopting a volume-based pricing model. Instead of charging businesses based on how many contacts they store in their database (the standard model used by Mailchimp), Brevo charges purely based on the number of emails actually sent.

This architectural shift makes it an incredibly cost-effective engine for scaling ecommerce brands and digital agencies that possess massive lead lists but only email a segmented fraction of them at any given time. With built-in SMS capabilities and transactional email routing, it easily replaces multiple tools for a fraction of the cost.

Operational Advantages

Pay-per-email pricing model

Native SMS and WhatsApp tools

Unlimited contact storage

Known Limitations

Basic native CRM features

Template builder lacks advanced styling
